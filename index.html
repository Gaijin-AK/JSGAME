In the game of Snake, the player uses the arrow keys(or "WSAD" keys) to move a "snake" around the board. As the snake finds food, it eats the food, and thereby grows larger. The game ends when the snake either moves off the screen or moves into itself. The goal is to make the snake as large as possible before game over.



  • Click on "SNAKE" for the Rules
  • "W" or UP arrow to go UP
  • "S" or Down arrow to go DOWN
  • "A" or Left arrow to go LEFT
  • "D" or Right arrow to go RIGHT

Press any of the Control Keys to Start
